Panecake

Description

This delicious berry sauce for pancakes is made with blueberries and raspberries. Try this thick, rich topping on waffles too.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups frozen raspberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Combine the raspberries, blueberries, sugar, and water in a small saucepan
  2. bring to a boil and cook at a boil for 5 minutes, scraping the bottom as needed to keep from burning
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer until thick, about 10 minutes
  4. Serve warm
